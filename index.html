About

A bit about me

I'm a DevOps engineer based in rural Utah with a passion for cloud infrastructure, automation, and CI/CD pipelines. I enjoy building systems that are reliable, scalable, and maintainable.

Outside of work I run a self-hosted homelab on my own infrastructure — managing containerized services with Terraform and Podman, exposing them securely via Cloudflare Tunnel, and monitoring everything with Prometheus and Grafana.

When I'm not at the keyboard, I'm enjoying video games, good food, and traveling with my wife Megan.
